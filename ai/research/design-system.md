# Design System — foundationrepairinsurance.com

## Vibe
Earthy, trustworthy, structural. LIGHT and bright corporate — white/stone backgrounds.

## Colors
- Primary "Slate Steel": #2C4A6E (structural/trustworthy)
- Accent "Clay Amber": #D4722A (earth/construction)
- White: #FFFFFF | Light stone: #F5F3EF | Warm gray: #EDEAE5
- Text charcoal: #1C1C1E

## Typography
- Headings: Montserrat (700/800, structural)
- Body: Source Sans 3 (Source Sans Pro)

## Motifs
- Soil-layer horizontal rule lines, geometric grid, foundation/structure shapes
- Heavy construction equipment imagery
- Subtle reveal-on-scroll, lenis smooth scroll

## Components
- Light cards on stone bg, slate headers, amber CTAs
- Trust bar, stat counters, accordion FAQ, testimonial cards
